Frequently Asked Questions

What prerequisites are required for this course?

Basic IT knowledge and familiarity with cloud concepts are recommended. Experience in retail IT is beneficial but not mandatory. No specific Azure experience is required, as we'll cover everything from the ground up.

How long does the program take to complete?

The standard program is designed to be completed in 9-10 weeks, with about 40-45 hours of instruction spread over that period. We offer flexible options including weekend bootcamps for those who prefer a more intensive schedule.

Is certification included in this program?

While this program thoroughly prepares you for Azure certifications, the actual certification exams are not included in the course fee. However, we provide guidance on which certifications (such as Azure Administrator or Azure Solutions Architect) align best with your career goals in retail IT.

How is this program different from general Azure courses?

Our program is specifically tailored for retail IT professionals. All examples, case studies, and hands-on projects are based on real-world retail scenarios. We cover topics like e-commerce platform deployment, inventory management systems, and customer data analytics using Azure services.
